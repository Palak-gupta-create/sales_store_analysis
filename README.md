# 📊 Sales Store Analysis

## 🏬 Store Background
A retail store chain tracks daily sales transactions, including order details, customer information, product categories, order time, and order status.

The business aims to leverage this data to:
- Optimize operations  
- Improve customer experience  
- Increase overall profitability  

---

## ❗ Problem Statement
Currently, the store lacks clear insights into its data, leading to several business challenges:

- Unable to identify top-selling products  
- No clear understanding of customer preferences  
- Difficulty in determining which products generate the most profit  
- Lack of visibility into delivery and operational issues  

👉 Due to these gaps:
- Revenue opportunities are being missed  
- Customer satisfaction is decreasing  
- Business decisions are not data-driven  

---

## 💡 Solution
To overcome these challenges, a data analysis solution is proposed:

- Create interactive dashboards and reports  
- Analyze sales trends and product performance  
- Identify high-value customers and buying patterns  
- Track delivery performance and operational bottlenecks  

---

## 📈 Key Insights (Example)
- Top-performing products and categories  
- Revenue and profit analysis  
- Customer segmentation  
- Order status analysis (Delivered, Cancelled, Pending)  
- Peak sales time analysis  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI 📊  
- SQL 🗄️  
- Excel 📑  

---

## 🎯 Project Objective
The main goal of this project is to convert raw sales data into meaningful insights that help in:
- Better decision-making  
- Increasing revenue  
- Improving customer satisfaction  

---

## 🚀 Outcome
- Improved visibility of sales performance  
- Better understanding of customer behavior  
- Identification of operational issues  
- Data-driven business strategy
